NYC Math State Test Scores

Looking at the progression of math state test scores in each district from 2013-2023, with breakdowns of the financial situation of test takers in each district, to understand how financial situation may play a role in education, and to see where the more underserved communities are in NYC

What counts as economically disadvantaged? - A student who participates in, or has a family member that participates in, economic assistance programs (such as reduced-price or free lunch programs, food stamps, foster care, EITC, SSI, TANF, or others) are identified as economically disadvantaged
